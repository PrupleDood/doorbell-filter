Doorbell Filter

Doorbell Filter AI is a machine learning model that is designed to distinguish residents, packages, and unknown people. It is trained on over 10,000 images to recognize humans, packages, and faces. This ensures you are not alerted for small movements which could take away from the important notifications like breakins or package theft.

Try our Demo

Reliable and Accurate

Our model performs at 93% accuracy in testing, demonstrating its high performance and ability to filter the potential threats from those which arent.

Free to Use

Our product is completely free to use, allowing people to streamline their existing doorbells.

Not entirely autonomous

Despite the high accuracy of our model, it is not foolproof, and it can malfunction. Also, the entire streamlining process is pointless if you don't check notifications. The model is no true substitute for diligence.

Four Unique Classes of Object Detection

This model is able to classify People, Package, Faces and the Faces of residents.

ViT

Doorbell Filter AI utilizes a Convolutional Neural Network model for its training and testing, using Roboflow.
